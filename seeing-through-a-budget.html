I love to explore visualizations with code. We are in the age of interactive information and there is nothing cooler than coming up with a complicated algorithm to simplify an idea. While there are many algorithms I created in this Sankey diagram, my favorite was for the font size of the text. Just like a map has different fonts sizes depending on the size of locations, I created a method that calculated the relative value of a node in relation to surrounding nodes. That then determined the size of the font.
