Since I have a passion for helping researchers present their work to wider audiences, I have focused on becoming an expert in D3 and data visualization. Believing manipulation of data was as important if not more so, I created a platform that relies on push based design and stateless elements. This allows me to add or take away features at will. My favorite example of this is in the GWAS Catalog below. Notice the smooth staggering of transitions as you add or take away elements from the visualization. Even if you change the animation speed mid transition the app does not break.
